Cover page — hand-drawn title: "When I Helped a New Friend"

🪑

Jamie sits alone at the lunch table. There is an empty seat beside them.

👀

A new student named Sam arrives and looks around the room nervously.

💭

Jamie notices Sam looking for somewhere to sit.

✏️

Jamie thinks: "Maybe I could say hello." A speech bubble is drawn above their head.

👋

Jamie takes a deep breath and gives a small wave across the table.

😊

Sam waves back and smiles. A big drawn smile fills the page.

🤝

Sam walks over and sits down next to Jamie at the table.

🥪

Jamie and Sam both open their lunch boxes and peek at what is inside.

😄

They both laugh at something funny. Wiggly laugh lines are drawn around them.

👍

The teacher walks past and gives Jamie a big thumbs up.

💛

At the end of the day, Jamie draws a heart in the middle of the page. They feel good inside.

🌟

Back cover — "The End. You did great, Jamie." Signed by the author.

Social Story · Carol Gray

When I Feel Worried

Sometimes I feel worried. This can happen when something new is about to happen, or when I am not sure what to do next.

When I feel worried, my tummy might feel strange. My heart might beat a little faster. These feelings are normal. They happen to most people.

1

Other people — like teachers, parents, and friends — sometimes feel worried too. They understand that feeling worried is okay.

When I feel worried, I can take a slow, deep breath. I can find a trusted person and let them know how I am feeling.

When I Feel Worried
Written for Liam, March 2026

Sometimes I feel worried. This can happen

when something new is about to happen, or

when I'm not sure what to do next.

 

When I feel worried, my tummy might feel

strange. My heart might beat faster.

These feelings are normal — most people

feel this way sometimes.

 

When I feel worried, I can take a slow breath.

I can find a trusted person. I can remember

that the worried feeling will pass.

 

I am doing really well. ✦
